# Wuxia Core Terms

This page provides a starter set of wuxia-oriented terminology.

| Chinese | Preferred English | Alternatives | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 江湖 | jianghu | martial world | often better kept as jianghu |
| 武林 | martial world | wuxia world | depends on tone |
| 掌门 | sect leader | sect master | lock one version |
| 长老 | elder | senior elder | hierarchy may vary |
| 内力 | inner force | internal energy | style choice |
| 轻功 | lightness skill | qinggong | both are common |
| 剑法 | sword art | sword technique | depends on naming style |
| 刀法 | blade art | saber technique | keep style aligned |
| 暗器 | hidden weapon | concealed weapon | common wuxia term |
| 武学 | martial art | martial discipline | broad category term |
