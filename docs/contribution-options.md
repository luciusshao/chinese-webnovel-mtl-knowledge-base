# Contribution Options

There are three practical ways to contribute.

## Option 1: Send One Term

Best for ordinary users.

Use:

- [Quick Term Submission Template](forms/quick-term-submission-template.md)

## Option 2: Send A Small Glossary

Best for readers who have 5 to 30 repeated terms.

Use:

- [Glossary Submission Template](forms/glossary-submission-template.md)
- [Batch Glossary Submission Template](forms/batch-glossary-submission-template.md)

## Option 3: Edit A Downloadable CSV

Best for users who prefer spreadsheet editing.

Use:

- [Glossary Library](glossary-library.md)
- [How To Download And Edit Glossaries](how-to-download-and-edit-glossaries.md)

## Easiest Path For Most People

If you do not know GitHub, start with the quick term template or a CSV download.
