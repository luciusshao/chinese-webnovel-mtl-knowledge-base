# How To Submit To The Maintainer

If you do not use GitHub directly, you can still contribute.

## Recommended Process

1. Open one of the plain-text templates.
2. Fill in your terms.
3. Send the text to the maintainer through the channel they provide.

## Best Submission Format

Use plain text like this:

```text
Genre: xianxia
Chinese term: 本命法宝
Preferred English: natal treasure
Reason: shorter and more common in cultivation discussions
Notes: use consistently for the same item category
```

## Good Small Contribution

Even one useful term is enough if:

- it appears repeatedly;
- the English choice is clearer;
- you explain why it should be preferred.

## What The Maintainer Will Do

The maintainer can:

- turn your text into a glossary issue;
- add it to an existing page;
- ask follow-up questions if the term is context-sensitive.
