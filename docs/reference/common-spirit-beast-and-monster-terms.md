# Common Spirit Beast And Monster Terms

Beast and monster vocabulary is common in xianxia, wuxia-adjacent fantasy, and cultivation novels.

| Chinese | Common English | Alternatives | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 妖兽 | demon beast | monster beast | very common category term |
| 灵兽 | spirit beast | spiritual beast | common contracted beast term |
| 凶兽 | ferocious beast | savage beast | tone depends on novel |
| 兽核 | beast core | monster core | resource item term |
| 妖丹 | demon core | beast core | depends on setting |
| 契约兽 | contracted beast | bond beast | companion term |
| 血脉 | bloodline | lineage | often beast or clan inheritance |
| 化形 | take human form | transform into human form | common beast progression term |
| 驯服 | tame | subdue | companion capture verb |
| 兽潮 | beast tide | monster tide | large-scale invasion term |

## Practical Rule

Monster taxonomy often becomes a progression system. Record recurring classes and rank ladders if they matter to plot logic.
