# Common Address And Honorifics

Address terms and honorifics are one of the fastest ways for MTL to feel awkward or inconsistent.

| Chinese | Common English | Alternatives | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 师父 | master | teacher | context-sensitive |
| 师尊 | honored master | revered master | more elevated than 师父 |
| 师兄 | senior brother | martial brother | often keep hierarchy explicit |
| 师姐 | senior sister | martial sister | depends on sect context |
| 师弟 | junior brother | martial junior brother | tone-sensitive |
| 师妹 | junior sister | martial junior sister | tone-sensitive |
| 前辈 | senior | elder senior | very common generic respectful term |
| 道友 | fellow Daoist | Dao friend | xianxia-specific social term |
| 公子 | young master | noble son | context varies |
| 姑娘 | miss | young lady | register-sensitive |
| 大人 | my lord | lord | official or deferential title |
| 陛下 | Your Majesty | Majesty | imperial register |

## Practical Rule

Choose whether you want to preserve hierarchical relationships explicitly. Once you choose, apply it consistently.
