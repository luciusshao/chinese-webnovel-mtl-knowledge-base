# Common Address And Honorifics

Address terms and honorifics are one of the fastest ways for MTL to feel awkward or inconsistent.

| Chinese | Common English | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 师父 | master | context-sensitive |
| 师尊 | honored master | more elevated than 师父 |
| 师兄 | senior brother | often keep hierarchy explicit |
| 师姐 | senior sister | depends on sect context |
| 师弟 | junior brother | tone-sensitive |
| 师妹 | junior sister | tone-sensitive |
| 前辈 | senior | very common generic respectful term |
| 道友 | fellow Daoist | xianxia-specific social term |
| 公子 | young master | context varies |
| 姑娘 | miss | register-sensitive |
| 大人 | my lord | official or deferential title |
| 陛下 | Your Majesty | imperial register |

## Practical Rule

Choose whether you want to preserve hierarchical relationships explicitly. Once you choose, apply it consistently.
