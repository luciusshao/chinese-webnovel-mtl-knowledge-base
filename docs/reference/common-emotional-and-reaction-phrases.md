# Common Emotional And Reaction Phrases

These phrases show up constantly in serialized fiction and often sound unnatural in raw MTL.

| Chinese | Common English | Alternatives | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 冷哼一声 | snorted coldly | let out a cold snort | very common narration phrase |
| 脸色一变 | his expression changed | his face changed | smoother English preferred |
| 倒吸一口凉气 | sucked in a cold breath | gasped sharply | common shock reaction |
| 心中一惊 | was startled inwardly | inwardly startled | internal reaction phrase |
| 杀意 | killing intent | murderous intent | both common |
| 气息 | aura | presence | cultivation context matters |
| 威压 | pressure | oppressive aura | common xianxia force term |
| 神色复杂 | expression complicated | complicated expression | often needs smoothing |
| 目瞪口呆 | dumbfounded | stunned speechless | common reaction phrase |
| 若有所思 | thoughtful | as if deep in thought | repeated prose pattern |

## Practical Rule

Phrase-level consistency matters because serialized novels reuse the same reactions heavily.
