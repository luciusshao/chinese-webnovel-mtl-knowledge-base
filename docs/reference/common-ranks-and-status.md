# Common Ranks And Status Terms

Ranks, talent labels, and status terms appear everywhere in progression and martial stories.

| Chinese | Common English | Alternatives | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 天才 | genius | prodigy | common talent label |
| 妖孽 | monster | monstrous genius | often metaphorical praise |
| 废物 | trash | waste | insult term, tone-sensitive |
| 外门弟子 | outer disciple | outer court disciple | sect hierarchy term |
| 内门弟子 | inner disciple | inner court disciple | sect hierarchy term |
| 核心弟子 | core disciple | central disciple | common rank term |
| 亲传弟子 | direct disciple | personal disciple | lineage-important term |
| 候选人 | candidate | nominee | context-sensitive |
| 天骄 | favored heaven talent | heavenly pride | difficult prestige term |
| 圣子 | Holy Son | Saint Son | lock one style |
| 圣女 | Holy Maiden | Saintess | lock one style |

## Practical Rule

Rank labels often encode social structure. Treat them as system terms, not decorative wording.
