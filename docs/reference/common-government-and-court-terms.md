# Common Government And Court Terms

Historical fantasy, court intrigue, and kingdom-building stories rely on official titles that raw MTL often mangles.

| Chinese | Common English | Alternatives | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 皇帝 | emperor | sovereign | default is emperor |
| 皇后 | empress | empress consort | context-sensitive |
| 太子 | crown prince | heir apparent | common succession title |
| 公主 | princess | imperial princess | rank context matters |
| 王爷 | prince | imperial prince | avoid random variation |
| 丞相 | chancellor | prime minister | setting-dependent |
| 尚书 | minister | secretary | depends on bureaucracy style |
| 将军 | general | military commander | military office |
| 朝廷 | imperial court | court | broad political term |
| 圣旨 | imperial decree | decree | common authority phrase |

## Practical Rule

Court stories depend heavily on hierarchy. Once you choose a title mapping system, keep it stable across the whole cast.
