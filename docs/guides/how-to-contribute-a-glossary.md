# How To Contribute A Glossary

## Good Glossary Contributions

A good glossary contribution does more than dump terms. It should explain:

- the genre or novel context;
- which terms must stay fixed;
- which terms have acceptable alternatives;
- why a specific English choice is preferred.

## Minimum Suggested Format

Include:

- 10 to 30 recurring terms;
- one sentence about scope;
- notes for ambiguous terms;
- a consistent naming strategy.

## Example Scope Statements

- early-stage xianxia cultivation terms;
- sect and faction naming conventions;
- historical court titles;
- demon beast and alchemy terminology.
