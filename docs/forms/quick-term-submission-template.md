# Quick Term Submission Template

Use this if you only want to submit one term or a few terms.

```text
Genre:

Chinese term:

Preferred English:

Alternatives:

Reason:

Notes:
```

## Example

```text
Genre: wuxia
Chinese term: 江湖
Preferred English: jianghu
Alternatives: martial world
Reason: preserves the cultural feel better in most wuxia contexts
Notes: martial world is acceptable in explanatory contexts
```
